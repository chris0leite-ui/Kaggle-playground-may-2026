## One-line conclusion

**The host's data-generating pipeline is now characterised at the
structural level**: input → custom sampling weights → per-cell
continuous generator (the unsolved residual) → structured Driver/Stint
fabrication → output. The remaining 0.22 gap to perfect mimicry is
the per-cell continuous-density generator, which is empirically NOT in
SDV's library and NOT explicable by simple analytic alternatives.

variant — all untested and queued for next session.
