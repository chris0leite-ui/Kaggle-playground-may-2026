## Why this is the most important data point

This proves the rank-lock isn't from "LR-meta missing interactions" or
"too few features." The 12-feat [P, rank, logit] expansion already
spans the predictive subspace. Polynomial expansion is mathematically
strictly richer (LR can fit any subset of the 78 added features), but
it produces the SAME OOF (within noise band).

**Rank-lock is structural to the K=4 base-prediction information
content**, not a fitting limitation. The 1.33-rank logit subspace
contains all the predictive signal the bases jointly produce. No
polynomial transform of those bases recovers a missing direction.
