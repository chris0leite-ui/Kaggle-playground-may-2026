   Audit §5.3 ("mechanism-class-only") was correctly prioritized
   over LR-meta tweaks. F5/B (meta-layer changes) falsified; F1.2

   (~0.946) but the residual structure is genuinely orthogonal.
   Driver × Compound (R3) at ρ=0.891 is the most diverse base

   OOF; K=18 with 4 rules gave +0.78bp OOF and +2.1bp LB

   in the OOF→LB transfer).

   M5q's gap was −5.2bp; K=18's gap is −3.9bp. The OOF→LB ratio
   improved, opposite of d5 partial-pseudo's gap-widening pattern.

## Friction additions for `audit/friction.md`

- `tag: pre_submit_diff_csv_precision_loss` — pre_submit_diff.py
  measures Spearman ρ on CSV-roundtripped float32 vs the build
  script's float64 in-memory arrays. At ρ ≈ 0.999, the CSV
  precision loss can flip the verdict (script said ρ=0.99902 PASS;
  CSV said 0.99902 = 0.999021 TIE). Fix: bump the tie threshold
  to 0.9995 OR run pre_submit_diff on the in-memory test arrays
  (oof_*_strat.npy + test_*_strat.npy) instead of CSV. Update
  scripts/pre_submit_diff.py default ref logic.
