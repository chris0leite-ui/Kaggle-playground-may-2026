# Day-13a — Move B variant 1: 5/3 multi-FM partition

**Branch**: `claude/review-ml-handover-VTvWw` (parallel to main's d13_path_b work).

## Hypothesis

A 5-driver-state / 3-race-context FM partition is a different operating
point than d9f's 4/4 (D,C,S,T vs R,Y,Rp,P) and d9i's 6/6 augmented.
Different shape may carry orthogonal signal that LR-meta routes around
existing FMs. Predicted: standalone FM_A_53 ≈ 0.83-0.85, ρ vs PRIMARY
0.55-0.65, marginal stack +0.1-0.5bp pred-OOF; LB amplification per
d9h/d9i pattern.

## Build

- **FM_A_53** (5 driver-state): D, C, S, T_q5, **Cd_q5**
- **FM_B_53** (3 race-context): R, Y, Rp_q5
- Excludes P (driver-outcome) and Ld (driver-state outcome) — overlap
  with T/Cd hurts FM_A interaction lattice
- Hyperparams identical to d9c/d9f/d9i (k=8, 6 epochs, LR=0.05) —
  d9d sweep was flat
- Stacks vs PRIMARY (d9i S1 K=21 swap, OOF 0.95071 / LB 0.95034):
  - S1 K=21 swap (drop d9c FM, add 5/3)
  - S2 K=23 add (alongside d9f FM_A+B)
  - S3 K=24 add+ (keep d9c too; all 5 FMs present)

## LB result

**Submitted S3 K=24** (lowest ρ + broadest pool) → LB **0.95032**
(TIE / −0.02bp regress vs d9i PRIMARY 0.95034). FM-class amplification
(d9h 300×, d9i flip) DID NOT FIRE.

## Verdict

- ✓ Both FMs are leakage-robust under GKF
- ✓ FM_A_53 is the most-diverse FM_A measured against current PRIMARY
- ✗ Strat stack TIE_EXPECTED — partition shape alone doesn't lift LB
- ✗ FM-class amplification asymmetry didn't fire on same-field reshuffle

Confirms (with d13b/c/d) that the **partition-shape axis is saturated**
within the same 12-field set. Future FM lift needs new INPUT signal,
not new partitions.
