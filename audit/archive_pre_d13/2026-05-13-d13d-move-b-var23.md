# Day-13d — Move B variants 2 + 3: partition-shape axis SATURATED

**Branch**: `claude/review-ml-handover-VTvWw`.

## Hypothesis

After d13a's 5/3 was TIE on Strat, the question is whether OTHER
partition shapes can break the rank-lock. Two more variants from
the HANDOVER Day-13 plan:

- **Variant 2** (4/4 Compound × TyreLife axis): different
  orthogonalisation than d9f. FM_A on wheel-physics fields,
  FM_B on categorical-identity.
- **Variant 3** (6/6 alt-split): different from d9i's 6/6.
  T moves to FM_B, Nx/Pv move to FM_A — re-pairs neighbour-compound
  signal with driver/compound rather than race-context.

## Build

- **V2** — 4/4 CT-axis:
  - FM_A_CT: C, T, Cd, Ld (wheel-physics)
  - FM_B_DR: D, R, S, Y (categorical-identity)
- **V3** — 6/6 alt-split:
  - FM_A_DH: D, C, Cd, Ld, Nx, Pv (driver+history+neighbours)
  - FM_B_RT: R, Y, S, T, Rp, P (race+position+tire-life)
- Stacks added on T1_drop_d9c K=23 baseline (post-Move C minimal):
  - V2_K25 (add FM_A_CT + FM_B_DR)
  - V3_K25 (add FM_A_DH + FM_B_RT)
  - V2+V3_K27 (all 4 new FMs)

PRIMARY for ρ comparison: d9i_S1_K21_swap_aug2way (Strat 0.95071, LB 0.95034).

## Findings

1. **V2 (CT-axis) regresses** — wheel-physics partition is redundant
   given d9f + d13a already cover (D,C,S,T) and (D,C,S,T,Cd).

2. **V3 marginal +0.03bp** — within bp-resolution noise floor.
   The FM-A field ordering (Nx/Pv with driver vs race) doesn't
   shift the meta routing.

3. **V2+V3 combined REGRESSES** vs V3 alone — K-budget saturates
   around K=23-25 on this pool. Adding bases past that point hurts.

4. **NONE of 4 new FMs lands in top-8 L1** in any stack. Meta routing
   demotes them below rules + GBDTs + d9 cohort.

## Cross-shape summary

Across **6 FM partition shapes tested** (d9c unified, d9f 4/4,
d9h 12-field, d9i 6/6 aug, d13a 5/3, d13d V2 4/4 CT, d13d V3 6/6 alt),
every stack converges to **Strat 0.95073 ± 0 bp**. **Within-FM-class
diversification frontier on the partition-shape axis is exhausted**
at this pool size and field set.

## Implication

Future FM lift cannot come from re-partitioning the same 12-field
set. Either:
- (a) New input fields in the FM (Cd, Ld, Nx, Pv augmentation worked
  for d9h/d9i; need MORE such features — pit-window-since-last-pit,
  hazard-decay, compound-pressure)
- (b) New model class (DeepFM-lite, regularised FFM, TabPFN
  fine-tune, hier-meta layer)

Confirms main's parallel-agent V1/V2/V3 dead-listing. Public-LB
result for d13a S3 K=24 (LB 0.95032 TIE) gives an empirical
confirmation of the saturation claim.
