### Why the OOF→LB gap inverted

`oof_d12_lr_meta_strat.npy` is itself the K=21 LR-meta-OOF — a
convex combination of the same 21 bases that compose the K=21
pool. Adding it as a 22nd "base" creates a 2-level stack but
no truly new signal flow into the segment routers. The hier-meta
routed between the inner-meta-prediction and base-predictions,
gained +0.99 bp OOF AUC by exploiting OOF-fold structure, but
the inner-meta-prediction's calibration at test time (full-train
fit) is structurally different from its OOF (per-fold fit). The
test-time prediction did not preserve the OOF gain.

**Lesson (new friction tag candidate):** Path B family-conditional
amplification (8-11.6×) is conditional on the K_pool addition
carrying new/orthogonal SIGNAL (FM-class diverse vs GBDT-class;
sparse-LR diverse vs dense-LR; etc.), NOT on adding a meta-
derivative whose information is already in the pool.
