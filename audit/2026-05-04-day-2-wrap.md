  meta-stacker compensates for redundancy by over-fitting OOF noise.

  test is i.i.d. row split. GroupKF was diagnostic-only, never used
  to reject a candidate, costing ~50% extra compute per base. Skill

## Problems to address (load-bearing for Day 3)

1. **GBDT family near-saturated.** E3 HGBC (best single, 0.94870 LB)
   captures ~95% of available GBDT signal. β variants confirmed
   adding more HGBC clones ~99% correlated → no real diversity.
   Further GBDT-only stacks will gain diminishing returns.
2. **Gap-widening with redundancy.** Each new high-correlation base
   adds OOF noise that meta over-weights. Naive "more bases = better"
   strategy is broken. Day-3 must filter by pool-pairwise-correlation
   gate before adding.
3. **Leader gap (54bp) likely structural, not parameter.** Research-
   loop cited problem-reformulation diversity (objective-space) and
   pseudo-labeling as the recurring cross-comp lever. Both untried
   at scale; A/B were cheap probes that confirmed reformulation lifts
   in stack but not enough alone.
4. **No GPU access.** RealMLP CPU = 39.5min/fold; ζ deep CatBoost
   single-fold matched E3 at 5.7min but 5-fold pursuit not cost-
   justified (+1bp marginal vs E3 mean for 28min compute).
